Templates

Patterns - not cut and paste (honest)

uSDLC templates do not add anything except convention to the system. Any page anywhere can be a template holder. uSDLC comes installed with a template page at the global level. For many projects a private template set may also be of benefit.

A template can be created directly in a templates page, just as you would any other section. Alternatively you can copy and paste sections from other parts of the document.

To use a template, go to a template page, highlight a template and copy it from the drop-down menu or with ^C. It will stay in your clipboard until you paste it at the destination.
